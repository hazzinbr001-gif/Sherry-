🏥 About This App
🏥
Medical Survey System (MSS)
Ministry of Health · v4.2 · Multi-Institution Edition
The App
Medical Survey System (MSS) v6.0 is a multi-institution, offline-first Progressive Web App (PWA) built for community health fieldwork. Ministry of Health.

It was designed so that health workers can collect data in the field — even with zero internet — and generate official Ministry of Health format reports automatically. No paper, no spreadsheets, no stress. 🎉
The Developer
Ministry of Health Kenya — Development Team
A developer who believes that technology should solve real problems for real people — not just look cool in a portfolio. This app was built with genuine care for public health in Kenya, with a love for clean code and the firm belief that community health workers deserve better tools than a clipboard and a prayer.

When not debugging JavaScript at 2am, Brian can be found wondering why CSS always has one more issue to fix. 😄
What It Does
📋 12-Section Survey 📴 Works Offline ☁️ Auto Sync 📑 Official Reports 👥 Multi-User 🔐 Role-Based Access 📊 Admin Dashboard ⭐ Premium
Can This App Be Adapted?
Absolutely yes. This app was built to be adaptable. It can be customised for any Medical Survey System, any county, any university, or any public health programme in Kenya or beyond. Different survey sections, different questions, different report formats — all possible. Just reach out. The goal is better public health data for everyone. 🌍
Premium Platform
This platform supports community health data collection for institutions and their enumerators. Contact your institution admin to get access.

🆘 Help & Contact
Quick Help
🔑 Login Issues
Use your admission number in the format given by your university (e.g. B11/GLUK/S53K/2022). Make sure you are connected to the internet the first time you register. If your access has been removed, contact your course coordinator.
📴 Working Offline
The app works fully offline. All your interviews are saved on your phone automatically. When you get back to an area with internet, tap Upload Data to sync your records to the server.
📑 Generating Reports
After finishing an interview, tap Finish and choose your report type. For all your reports together, tap My Reports on the home page. To print as PDF, open the report then use your browser's Share → Print → Save as PDF.
🔄 App Not Updating?
If the app looks different from what others have, close it completely and reopen. The app updates itself in the background when you have internet. If still stuck, go to your browser settings → Clear site data → reopen.
Contact — Course Coordinator
For access problems or survey-related questions, contact your institution's admin or course coordinator. For app issues, contact the developer below.
